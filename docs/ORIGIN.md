# Origin

This repo exists because pipeline dashboards often show volume before they show friction.

The usual failure modes are familiar:
- demand generation creates enough names, but not enough accepted pipeline
- segment performance gets flattened into one blended view
- stage aging becomes visible only after a quarter is already at risk
- sourced and influenced pipeline tell different stories, but the reporting layer hides the difference

`pipeline-velocity-dash` was designed as a Kinetic Gain portfolio build to show how funnel visibility can stay commercially useful, not just visually polished.

The operating principle is simple:
- show the stage flow
- compare the segments
- surface the risks
- tie the slowdown back to revenue pressure
